# 2026-06-12 — [Ztor 2.0] eShop Phase-1 scope: physical goods only, Bundle supported

*Status: DRAFT — needs review*

## Decision
eShop 第一階段**只搬移實體商品**，**不包含**：虛擬商品、音樂、活動票券、會員卡。支援**綑綁包（Bundle）**：多件實體商品打包優惠價。音樂播放器、活動售票等功能列為**未來規劃**（視業務客戶需求而定）。

(English) eShop Phase 1 migrates **physical goods only**, **excluding**: virtual goods, music, event tickets, and membership cards. It supports **Bundles** — multiple physical goods packaged at a discounted price. Music player, event ticketing, etc. are **deferred to future planning**, contingent on business-client demand.

## Why
(not captured — scoping Phase 1 to physical goods + bundles keeps the first eShop migration tractable; richer product types follow demand.)
